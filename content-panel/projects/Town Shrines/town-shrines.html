Town Shrines

(2020)

Statement:

Town Shrines is a collection of "Bathtub Marys" and other various catholic shrines kept in private yards around Somerville, MA. The project originally started as a means to catalog the tone of being in a setting both religious, traditional, yet organized/centralized.

For a more anthropological undertaking see Deborah Pacini's work.;

Deborah Pacini

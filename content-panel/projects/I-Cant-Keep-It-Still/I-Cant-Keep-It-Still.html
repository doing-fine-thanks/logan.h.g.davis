I Can't Keep It Still

(2020 - Ongoing)

Statement:

I Can't Keep It Still is a collection of photos cataloging my traversal through places, or their movement past me. The mismatch of pace left between my perspective and the objects in frame documents an impression of them over anything that could be considered clinical.

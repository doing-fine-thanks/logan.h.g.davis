dev/random

(2017)

A collection of works done through text manipulation and (genetic) AI
generated imagery to display a computer's ability to produce randomness with intent
and with reckless abandon.

Randomness in interaction with art can either lend an emergent
uniqueness surprise for the artist or generate static that undermines
any conversation one can have with it. The art of making meaningful
work in the digital age is knowing an individual component's contribution to
this signal-to-noise ration of an entire piece.

(This show occurred from April 4th-9th, 2017)

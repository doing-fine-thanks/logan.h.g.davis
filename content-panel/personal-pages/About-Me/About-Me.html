Bio:

Hi, my name is Logan Davis. I'm an artist and software developer living in Austin, TX.

My work is in creating maps and networks that connect in ways other than time, and space; objects that act as polymorphic material for distinctly separate ides. I lean into occurrences of multiplicity, shared vernacular, or metaphor as a means to archive with less concern for hierarchy and more reverence for relation.

I graduated from Marlboro College in 2017 with a BA in Programming languages, was software researcher for Black Duck software, and have since been working as a systems architect for Pairity AI (recently acquired by Finvi).

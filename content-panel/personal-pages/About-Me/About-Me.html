Bio:

Hi, my name is Logan Davis. I'm an artist and software developer.

My practice is grounded in creating maps and networks that connect in ways other than time and space; objects that act as polymorphic material for distinctly separate ideas. I lean into occurrences of multiplicity, shared vernacular, and metaphor as a means to archive with less concern for hierarchy and more reverence for relation.

I graduated from Marlboro College in 2017 with a BA in Programming Languages and current work as a systems architect for Pairity AI (recently acquired by Finvi). I can be found making ceramics, photos, and multimedia projects in Austin, TX.
